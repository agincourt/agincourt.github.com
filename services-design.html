Design & User Experience

When people visit a website they have specific tasks in mind, even for casual entertainment sites like YouTube, people want to achieve something, in this case it may be finding a video they're interested in; this is what makes a website more than a series of pretty pictures.

This is also the case for web applications - productivity is linked directly to how quickly and efficiently people can complete tasks.

It's Agincourt's mission to deliver experiences on the web which are not only intuitive and efficient for users, but which achieve maximum satisfaction and are ultimately enjoyable to use!
